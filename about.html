  1. Amaran

    "Amaran" is a Tamil action drama film based on the real-life story of Major Mukund Varadarajan, a brave Indian Army officer who was martyred during the Qazipathri Operation in Shopian, Kashmir. The film follows Mukund's journey, from his college years and love story with Indhu Rebecca to his time serving in the army and his eventual sacrifice.

  2. Vettaiyan

    "Vettaiyan" (The Hunter) tells the story of a relentless police officer, Athiyan, known for his encounter killings, who investigates a government school teacher's murder. He initially takes down a drug supplier in an encounter, but later, the teacher is brutally raped and murdered. The film explores Athiyan's struggle to reconcile his morally ambiguous actions with the law and the investigation into the murder, which reveals a deeper conspiracy.

  3. Maharaja

    "Maharaja" is a 2024 Indian Tamil-language action thriller film directed by Nithilan Saminathan and starring Vijay Sethupathi, Anurag Kashyap, and Mamta Mohandas. The film follows a barber in Chennai who goes to the police station to retrieve a stolen dustbin, but the police discover his intentions are more complex. The movie premiered on June 12, 2024, and was theatrically released on June 14, 2024

  4. Indian 2

    "Indian 2" is a 2024 Tamil-language vigilante action film directed by S. Shankar, and is the sequel to the 1996 film "Indian". It stars Kamal Haasan, who reprises his role as Senapathy, a former freedom fighter turned vigilante, who battles corruption. The film also features Siddharth, Rakul Preet Singh, and Priya Bhavani Shankar.

  5. liger

    "Liger" is a 2022 Indian sports action film directed by Puri Jagannadh, starring Vijay Deverakonda and Ananya Panday. The film follows a young man from a slum in Mumbai who trains to become a professional MMA fighter, facing challenges and obstacles along the way.
